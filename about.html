Our Nation

India is a land of diverse cultures, languages, traditions, and landscapes. With a population of over 1.4 billion, it is the world's largest democracy.

Facts about India:

  • Capital: New Delhi
  • Official Languages: Hindi and English
  • States: 28, Union Territories: 8
  • Currency: Indian Rupee (INR)
  • National Animal: Bengal Tiger
  • National Bird: Peacock
  • National Flower: Lotus
